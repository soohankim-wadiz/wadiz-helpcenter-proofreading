When will the wadiz settlement take place?

Source: helpcenter.wadiz.kr – When will the wadiz settlement take place?
This guidance applies only to Korean manufacturers (manufacturers holding a Korean business registration certificate).

Settlement of Korean Makers

Funding and Pre-order projects in Korea are settled in two installments: an interim settlement and a final settlement. Among Pre-order types, 'Special Configuration' and 'Limited Edition' projects are paid out entirely as a single final settlement (100% of the settlement amount). Wadiz makes settlement payments every Monday, Wednesday, and Friday.

Funding / Pre-order (Domestic Only) Settlement

  1. Within 5 business days of the project's end date, we will email the interim settlement statement to the maker's registered email address.
  2. After you review the interim settlement statement, the interim settlement will be paid on the next available payment date (Monday, Wednesday, or Friday) following the date the statement was sent.
  3. Based on the delivery completion date, the final settlement statement will be sent 25 days later (plus 1 additional business day).
  4. The final settlement will be paid on the next available Wednesday or Friday following the date the final settlement statement is sent.
  5. The final settlement can only proceed after you ship the reward and enter the shipping details in the Maker Studio [Shipping/Refund Management] menu.
  6. For intangible rewards (membership, subscription, license, etc.) or combined intangible/tangible rewards, the delivery completion date is the date the reward's use is completed or its validity period expires.
  7. For intangible rewards such as classes/consultations or usage/accommodation rights, in domestic Funding or Pre-order projects, the settlement ratio varies based on the amount raised:
    • Under KRW 10 million raised: 80% interim settlement, 20% final settlement.
    • KRW 10 million or more raised: 60% interim settlement, 40% final settlement.
    • KRW 50 million or more raised: 60% interim settlement, 20% second settlement (once the reward becomes available), and 20% final settlement (once the availability period ends).

Pre-order (Special Configuration / Limited Edition) Settlement

  1. Based on the delivery completion date, the final settlement statement will be sent 25 days later (plus 1 additional business day).
  2. For intangible rewards (membership, subscription, license, etc.) or combined intangible/tangible rewards, the delivery completion date is the date the reward's use is completed or its validity period expires.
  3. For original digital content such as e-book PDF files or class lecture VOD files, the final settlement statement will be sent 25 days (or more) after the reward file is delivered (e.g., by email).
  4. The final settlement will be paid on the next available Wednesday or Friday following the date the final settlement statement is sent.
  5. Special Configuration and Limited Edition Pre-order projects are not eligible for interim settlement by default. If you'd like to receive an advance payment similar to Funding and standard Pre-order projects in Korea, you can apply for the Interim Settlement Service.
  6. For the Interim Settlement (Seonjeongsan) service, 60% of the settlement amount will be paid in advance, but only for projects approved after application. The advance payment amount may vary depending on internal policy, so please refer to the Interim Settlement Service Terms and Conditions for details.

Settlement Process for Class / Consultation / Accommodation Reward Projects

For projects offering classes/consultations or usage/accommodation rights as rewards, the settlement structure changes once the amount raised reaches KRW 50 million: 20% of the total settlement amount is paid only after the reward has been used or its validity period has expired.

Settlement of Global Makers

This guidance applies only to global manufacturers (manufacturers with business registration outside Korea). Overseas makers who have officially registered as a business operator in Korea, including agencies, will follow the same settlement method as Korean makers described above.

Global Maker: A manufacturer with a business license issued outside Korea. Starting with projects submitted after June 1, 2026, global manufacturers will follow the same settlement method as Korean makers.

Submission DateSettlement Policy
Before June 1, 2026Single post-settlement payment: 100% of the amount, minus refunds and fees, paid after the reward is shipped.
From June 1, 2026 If the final settlement amount is under KRW 10 million: settled entirely as one payment after shipment (minus fees, refunds, etc.).

If the final settlement amount is KRW 10 million or more: an interim settlement of 60% (minus fees) is paid first, followed by a final settlement adjusting for refunds and other deductions.

Settlement Process (Global Makers)

  1. The interim settlement statement is sent within 5 business days of the project's end date (viewable under Maker Studio > Settlement Details).
  2. Ship rewards via Maker Studio > Shipping/Refund Management so Wadiz can confirm delivery.
  3. Once all rewards are delivered, final settlement begins: the final settlement statement is sent within 10 business days after the 25-day refund period (starting from the delivery completion date) ends.

Settlement Schedule

Wadiz pays global makers (those with a business license outside Korea) on a weekly basis. Times are in Korea Standard Time; if the scheduled Wednesday is a public holiday, payment is made on the next business day. Applies to projects whose settlement statement was issued between Monday and Friday — payment date is the following Wednesday after the statement is issued.

Example schedule:
  • 2026.05.04 (Mon): Project ends
  • 2026.05.11 (Mon): Interim settlement statement sent
  • 2026.05.20 (Wed): Interim settlement paid
  • 2026.06.01 (Mon): Reward delivery completed
  • 2026.06.26 (Fri): Refund period ends
  • 2026.06.29 (Mon): Final settlement statement sent
  • 2026.07.08 (Wed): Final settlement paid

The above policy applies to projects submitted on or after June 1, 2026.
